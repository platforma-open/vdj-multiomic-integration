Initial release of the VDJ Multiomic Integration block. Aggregates per-cell feature data onto VDJ clonotypes: per-feature fractions and UMI counts, restriction index, breadth, dominant feature, and optional per-clonotype gene expression (mean/max) and dominant cell-type annotation. Reuses the dataset's scClonotypeKey axis verbatim and pools cells across samples (no sample axis).
